BIO

As a young adult, I cultivated a vision for a career in the dynamic realm of technology. Pursuing this passion, I earned an Associate's degree in Visual Communications with a specialization in Interactive Media. Post-graduation, I prioritized my family before directing my focus towards professional endeavors. Once I was ready to commence my journey in the workforce as a receptionist, I embraced every chance for growth and progression. This dedication propelled me into a Quality Assurance role within an esteemed app development team, igniting my aspiration for a future transition into a developer position.

After four years of working as a QA professional, I recognized the need to proactively seize opportunities for personal and professional growth. Motivated by this realization, I enrolled in Grand Circus' .NET full stack course, where I am currently immersed in mastering Object-Oriented Programming principles through hands-on project development.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I engage in activities that stimulate both my mind and body. I am an avid chess enthusiast, challenging myself to enhance my skills through online play and friendly matches with peers. Additionally, I maintain a disciplined running regimen, aiming to participate in multiple 5k races throughout the summer months. These hobbies not only invigorate me but also serve as platforms for setting and achieving personal milestones.

In my career aspirations, I am dedicated to securing a developer position where I can apply the evolving skills I am mastering, while also actively pursuing continuous learning and skill development. My objective is to not only contribute effectively to a team but also to continually increase my value as an asset. I am dedicated to a path of advancement, eagerly anticipating the achievement of future goals beyond this initial milestone.
